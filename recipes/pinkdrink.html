Starbucks Pink Drink Copycat Recipe

A light and refreshing beverage that features the sweet flavors of strawberry acai and coconut

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 ½ cup strawberries (sliced)
  • 2 cups sweetened coconut milk (chilled)
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 tbsp Starbucks VIA instant refreshers very berry hibiscus powder
  • 1 ½ tbsp sugar
  • ice

Vanilla Cold Foam (optional)

  • 1 cup non-fat milk
  • 2 pumps vanilla flavored syrup

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Slice your strawberries and place 1 cup in a blender with the water, very berry hibicus powder and sugar. Blend on high until combined.
  2. Set the rest of the strawberries aside for now.
  3. Add 1 cup of coconut milk and a handful of ice to each cup.
  4. Divide your strawberry blended mixture evenly into each cup and stir.
  5. Top with remaining sliced strawberries!
  6. (optional) Combine non-fat milk and vanilla syrup and with an immulsion blender, blend until foamy. Add to the top of each drink and serve!
